# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

Our project follows the Contributor Covenant to provide a positive and inclusive community.

The short version:
- Be respectful and kind. Harassment and discriminatory behavior are not tolerated.
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences.
- If you witness or experience behavior that violates this code, please contact the maintainers at antoniooreany@example.com.

For the full text, see: https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/
